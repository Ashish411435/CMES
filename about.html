About Us

At CMES Power Electrician, we are dedicated to delivering safe, reliable, and professional electrical services in Gurgaon. With years of hands-on experience, our expert electricians provide complete solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial needs — from wiring and inverter installation to AC repair, maintenance, and lighting setup.

We believe in building trust through quality workmanship, on-time service, and transparent pricing, ensuring your power systems function smoothly and safely. Whether it’s a small repair or a large project, CMES Power Electrician is your dependable partner for all electrical and AC services in Gurgaon.
